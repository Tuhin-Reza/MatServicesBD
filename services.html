Language Training in BD

Our language training facility is exceptional from other Japanese training centers. It differs from others in the teaching methodology and overall management of the course.

  • Courses from basic level to conversational level:
    • Japanese as a 3rd language, suitable for Manga and Anime lovers
    • Basic Japanese, suitable for N5 exam
    • Intermediate Japanese, suitable for N4 exam
    • Conversational Japanese, suitable for N3 Exam
  • Experienced teachers with Master's degrees in Japanese Language & Culture from the University of Dhaka
  • Monthly sessions with Native Japanese speakers to improve listening skills
  • Three formal mock tests and resolution sessions for all JLPT exams
